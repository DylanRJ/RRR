Education

In this page I will be demonstrating the global influence of roots music. How it has affected other genres, especially in the UK.

In these pictures we can see the global influence of roots music in the way that world-renowned artists have been appreciators of roots artistis and their music for generations. From the Beatles to Massive Attack.
Hover over the pictures to see how the different artists who have gained notoriety in popular music have a relationship with those from the roots discipline.
The global influence of roots music can be seen in the connections and popularity of the music not just amongst fans but other musicians. Here is an example of Bob Masrley rubbing shoulders with the Beatles.
The cross-cultural appeal of the music was another huge factor in the rise of roots music in the UK. Here we see a young Massive Attack in 1986 known as The Wild Bunch.
The origins of punk had links with reggae. Here we see punk band The Clash with Dub artist Mikey Dread in 1980.
More artists in the punk rock genre also took inspiration from those within roots music like John Lydon and Sid Vicious here seen with Don Letts.
